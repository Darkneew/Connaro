Important Gestures

It is important to get, as fast as possible, the important gestures, to stop as fast as possible the spread of the virus

Wash your hand

Wash your hand to keep them germ-free, with soap when you can

Call the hospital

Immediatly call the hospital if you have troubles to breath, or if your temperature rise

Have your bottle

Always keep your bottle of water with you, and do not share it

Throw tissues

Don't use tissues more than once, and make sure to throw them

Avoid contact

Try avoiding to shake hands or kiss people

Sneeze in your elbow

Sneeze and cough in your elbows, not your hands.
